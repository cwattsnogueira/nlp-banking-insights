Banking Complaints – Sentiment Insights Dashboard

Overview

This dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of customer complaints submitted to banks in 2023. Using Natural Language Processing and sentiment analysis, we uncover patterns across products, regions, and time to support strategic decisions.

1. Overall Sentiment Distribution

Quick snapshot of how customers feel across all complaints.

2. Sentiment Distribution by Banking Product

Shows how sentiment varies across different banking products.

3. Sentiment Ratio by Product

Highlights the percentage of positive vs. negative sentiment per product.

4. Complaint Volume by Product (Treemap)

Visual hierarchy of complaint volume across products.

5. Product Risk Bubble Chart

Combines volume and negativity to identify high-risk products.

6. Sentiment Distribution by State

Shows how sentiment varies geographically across the U.S.

7. Top 10 States by Negative Sentiment Rate

Identifies regions with the highest proportion of negative feedback.

8. Monthly Complaint Volume

Tracks how complaint volume changes over time.

9. Sentiment Trends Over Time

Reveals how customer sentiment evolves month by month.

Business Insights

  • Products like Checking accounts and Credit cards show high complaint volume and negative sentiment.
  • Some products (e.g. Student loans) have more positive than negative feedback, indicating good service or resolution.
  • States like California and Florida show elevated negativity, suggesting regional service issues.
  • Complaint volume spikes in certain months may reflect policy changes or seasonal demand.
